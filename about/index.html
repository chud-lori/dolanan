Dolanan

Casual games for hanging out with friends.

What is this

A single installable web app bundling casual local-multiplayer games — chess, ludo, connect four, werewolf, battleship, hangman, congklak, and more — designed for pass-the-device or one-screen hotseat play. Fully offline after the first visit, no accounts, no backend, no telemetry.

Principles

  • Offline-first — works after first visit
  • No accounts, no tracking
  • English + Indonesian + Javanese
  • Light + dark mode, mobile-first
  • Sound + haptics, screen wake-lock

Built with

Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS. No framework. ~900 KB total. Service Worker for offline.
